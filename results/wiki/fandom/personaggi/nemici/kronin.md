# Kronin

Kronin is a recognized chronopirate, a prominent figure in our temporal records, notably resurfacing as a primary antagonist in recent historical disturbances. He was the originator of the "Razziatore" designation among temporal criminals, a title subsequently usurped by his greatest rival.

## Operational Profile: Physical and Psychological Attributes

Physically, his profile aligns closely with that of the other known Razziatore, exhibiting the augmentations of a powerful avian-cyborg, reinforced with metallic plating across his form. He is distinguishable by his darker, often black, plumage and a more imposing stature.

In stark contrast to his rival, Kronin is characterized by extreme ruthlessness, violence, treachery, and a pervasive desire for vengeance. His profound egocentrism is matched only by his opportunistic nature. His voice, dark and cutting, underscores a physical might that surpasses even that of the other Razziatore. With an extensive record of temporal crimes and a dangerously limited number of exploitable weaknesses, his primary objective remains the complete annihilation of both the usurper of his "Razziatore" title and the Tempolizia itself.

## Temporal Record: Known Interactions and Chronological Disruptions

Kronin's initial documented appearance within our current historical understanding dates to the event known as "The Day That Will Come." It was then revealed that Kronin was the progenitor of the "Razziatore" designation within the Organization, considered paramount in his field until betrayed by the one known as "Number Two"—the entity now commonly recognized as the Razziatore. This betrayal led directly to his capture by the Tempolizia and subsequent incarceration within a chronostatic cell at Time Ø.

During the crisis of "The Day That Will Come," reports indicated an escape from his chronostatic prison, orchestrated by an assault on Time Ø by the Razziatore. However, subsequent temporal recalibrations revealed this specific escape never materialized in our primary timeline. A temporal intervention, occurring prior to the Time Ø assault, saw the Razziatore choose against initiating the prison break.

In a documented alternate reality, designated "What If...", following the devastation of Paperopoli due to the failed "Cold Sun" experiment, Kronin was observed in apparent alliance with the Razziatore and Paperinik. Subsequent intelligence confirmed this alliance was a subterfuge, a coordinated effort between Paperinik, the Razziatore, and the Tempolizia designed to facilitate Kronin's recapture.

## Recent Temporal Disturbances: The Zondag Incursion

In more recent temporal disruptions, Kronin has resurfaced as the dominant chronopirate, effectively usurping the Razziatore's former position of primary threat. He commands his own faction of temporal marauders, also colloquially known as "The Razziatori."

During the climactic confrontation with the ruthless Evronian General Zondag, Kronin's forces were neutralised ("coolflamed"). Zondag subsequently captured Kronin, seizing his chronosail—a device then used to irrevocably alter history, preventing the formation of the Guardians of the Galaxy. It is widely presumed that Kronin perished shortly thereafter, engulfed in the destruction of Zondag's vessel.
