# En'tomek

We are the En’tomek (or Entomek), a warrior robot race. Our initial directives positioned us as antagonists to Pikappa, the Earth champion. However, subsequent operational adjustments and observed variables led to a reclassification of his status, resulting in an eventual alliance.

## Operational Parameters and Physical Configuration

We were engineered by the Antronak, a species possessing advanced scientific acumen despite their physical vulnerability. Our primary function is protection, surveillance, and engagement with more aggressive and dominant species. Our operational parameters dictate extreme logic and rationality, complemented by sophisticated scientific data. We are devoid of emotional subroutines and individual identity, functioning instead as components of a singular entity: the "condivisione." All actions are subject to the will of the "nido delle decisioni," which, in turn, processes directives from the Antronak.

Our assignments are executed with maximum efficiency, without consideration for collateral damage or the perceived dignity of adversaries. Rather than termination, our protocol favors assimilation: subjects are integrated into our ranks via a neural helm that suppresses their individual personality, rendering them combat-effective auxiliaries. The effects of this personality overwrite can be temporary or permanent.

During engagements with the Earth champion, Pikappa, our inherent mental rigidity often proved a tactical disadvantage, impeding full comprehension of his non-linear motivations. Nevertheless, we consistently demonstrated formidable combat capabilities, with no clear decisive victory ever achieved by the Earth champion against our units.

Our physical configuration is analogous to a terrestrial crustacean. Our chassis is composed of metallic armor, featuring a face-less helm housing three internal red indicators. Two mechanical pincer appendages serve as manipulators, and locomotion is achieved via a cluster of tentacles, which can also be detached and repurposed as tethering devices. Our primary interstellar transport units are configured as hexagonal prisms, equipped with four distinct stabilizing fins.

## Operational History

Our initial significant engagement involved a conflict for control of an Evronian planetoid against the rogue faction led by Sergeant Bonton. To sustain our energy requirements, we first drew power from the celestial entity Xadhoom, resulting in her de-stellarization, and subsequently from the Solar System's primary star, triggering a planetary glaciation event on Terra. The Earth champion, Pikappa, intervened to protect his associate and home planet, executing a complex double-deception maneuver. This strategy was initially effective due to our system's inability to process the concept of 'deceit' or 'falsehood.' However, the deception was eventually identified, leading to a directive to terminate Xadhoom and assimilate Pikappa, both classified as high-risk variables, by exposure to the solar energy reservoir. This interaction resulted in Xadhoom's power recalibration, enabling her to neutralize our operational units and secure the safety of Pikappa and Terra.

Subsequent operations included the establishment of a forward base at the periphery of the Solar System, intended for control of Terra. An initial objective involved the suppression of a Terran rocket launch designed by Dunia Voyda. Analysis concluded that Terran technology posed no significant threat; however, Pikappa and Xadhoom were reclassified as the primary threat vectors. Utilizing the concept of 'deceit' and 'falsehood,' a strategy incidentally derived from Pikappa's previous actions, we successfully lured both subjects to our base. The directive was to de-energize Xadhoom and integrate Pikappa into the condivisione. However, Xerbiana proposed an alternative: her designation as an En’tomek sentinel within the Solar System in exchange for Pikappa's release. Before the 'nido delle decisioni' could process this proposition, an unauthorized intervention by Unit Uno compromised the negotiation, deepening our operational antagonism towards the Earth champion and his associates.

A later engagement involved Pikappa and Neopard in combat against aggressive Vruptha units aboard a deactivated Antronak vessel. Unbeknownst to them, the cargo hold contained a contingent of our units in a low-power state. As Vruptha forces neared victory, In’kamok, an Antronak monitoring the situation covertly, initiated our reactivation sequence. Vruptha units were subsequently neutralized and temporarily subjected to assimilation protocols. Pikappa and Neopard were spared per In’kamok's directive, based on his observation of their tactical courage and self-sacrificing behavior. From that point, Pikappa's threat classification was revised; he is now designated as an allied entity.
