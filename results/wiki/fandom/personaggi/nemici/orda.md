# The Horde

The Horde is the feared name given to a fearsome collective of warriors, the eternal adversaries of the Guardian, Pikappa. These seven mighty Coronian warriors serve none other than the dreaded Moldrock.

## Character and Appearance

Their ranks are filled with terror:

*   **Egor**, the winged scout, commands the very winds and air, soaring above the battlefield to whisper secrets of enemies back to his master. He is the only one among them to wield true elemental power.
*   **Sharkan**, master of brutal combat, wields spiked, electrified maces that crackle with deadly intent.
*   **Goraz**, whose thunderous blows come from a massive, electrified warhammer, striking fear into the hearts of all who stand against him.
*   **Ragnar**, with his potent electrified spear, capable of cleaving through any defense.

Three other warriors complete their number, their names uttered only in hushed whispers, their ferocity no less terrifying.

## History

### The Past

Their dark legend begins shrouded in mystery. For a time, the warriors of the Horde existed only as colossal statues within Moldrock's formidable palace. Yet, a past was revealed: once, they were young, but already formidable and armed for conquest. Following their master, the young Coronian duck, as he mastered the terrifying black ray, they marched upon Corona. No fortress, no defense, could withstand their relentless advance, their dominion spreading rapidly across other kingdoms and civilizations.

However, their reign of terror was not absolute. Everett Ducklair, the great Scientist, and the Coronian scholars, through a cunning ploy, managed to imprison the Horde, sealing them away within the confines of the pentadimensional universe. In that strange, twisted reality, Moldrock's immense power waned. In a desperate act of loyalty, his warriors willingly sacrificed their very life essence, their most precious possession, to fuel him, believing they would be freed and renewed upon their escape from that prison-world. But even this desperate offering was in vain. The Horde remains bound to Moldrock, their thoughts and accusations a constant torment within him, for they have truly become one.

### The Clash with Pikappa

Bound within Moldrock, the Horde remained dormant until their master found a way to escape his pentadimensional prison, disguised as a Raksaka. Though the terrifying act of their transmigration from his very being was not witnessed, their re-emergence was explosive. Sharkan and Goraz burst forth into Dhasam-Bul, sowing chaos and panic as they relentlessly hunted for the hated Everett Ducklair. The Guardian, Pikappa, confronted them, and later, Ragnar joined the fray as Pikappa desperately sought Moldrock. It was Egor, with his unique control over the winds, who finally caught the Guardian off guard.

When the Horde laid siege to Paperopoli, their master commanded the destruction of a symbol, the great Money Bin itself. But on the Collina Ammazzamotori, Ragnar, caught attempting to crush the Guardian, was struck down by a cannonball fired by Scrooge McDuck himself! Ultimately, the Guardian, with the crucial aid of Everett Ducklair, Trauma, and the venerable Prior, managed to avert utter catastrophe. The Prior, wielding ancient powers, compelled the Horde back into the pentadimensional universe, where, eventually, Moldrock himself joined them once more. When Moldrock resurfaced on Corona, his formidable warriors remained sealed away, and for now, the dark saga of the seven terrifying warriors of the Horde appears to be held captive.
