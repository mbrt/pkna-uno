Among Moldrock's most fearsome enforcers, Ragnar stands as a formidable warrior of the Horde.

## Appearance

His very form appears chiseled from marble, clad in grey and brown armor that includes a full helmet, leaving only his face visible. He wields a terrifying, long spear, crackling with electricity, its double blade capable of separating at his command.

## History

For ages, Ragnar served among Moldrock's seven elite warriors of the Horde, until Everett, through his unimaginable power, banished him to the pentadimensional universe. Yet, Moldrock, ever cunning, held Ragnar's very life force within himself for millennia, and now, he has unleashed him once more to serve his dark schemes of vengeance.

He made his terrifying debut during the initial incursions into the caves surrounding the Dhasam-Bul monastery. Pikappa, having just overcome Sharkan and Goraz, was caught completely off guard by Ragnar's sudden manifestation. Later, Ragnar, alongside Goraz, launched a devastating assault on Scrooge McDuck's vault, pushing Pikappa to his limits. The hero barely escaped Ragnar's grasp, saved only by his uncle's quick thinking, who distracted the warrior with a direct cannon blast to the head. Ultimately, Ragnar, along with three other warriors, succumbed to the psychic might of the Evronian General Trauma, and was banished back to his dimension by the Prior of Dhasam-Bul.
