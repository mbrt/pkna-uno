# Miss Fingelbauer

Miss Fingelbauer serves as the personal secretary to Everett Ducklair, operating directly from his headquarters within the formidable Ducklair Tower.

She first came to prominence during the chilling events of *Solo un po' di paura* (PK² #2), and was later briefly involved in *Altovolume* (PK² #10) and *Affari di famiglia* (PK² #18).

## Aspetto

She is a tall, slender anthropomorphic duck of striking appearance, consistently seen in a form-fitting, low-cut blue dress. Her distinctive feature is her voluminous, wavy red hair, always complemented by an array of presumably gold jewelry.

Despite her professional facade, she has been identified as a significant adversary, often counted among the enemies of Pikappa.
