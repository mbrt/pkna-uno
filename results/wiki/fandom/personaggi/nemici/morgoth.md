# Morgoth

Morgoth appeared as a formidable adversary, later becoming an unexpected ally to Pikappa within the confines of what we now know was a simulated reality – the very dimension in which our conflict unfolded. He was, in essence, a construct designed to oppose.

## Character and Appearance

Initially, Morgoth's motivations seemed simplistic, driven solely by the relentless pursuit of conflict against Pikappa, much like a programmed obstacle. His existence appeared defined by these endless confrontations, lacking any deeper purpose. Yet, as events unfolded, a profound shift occurred within him; he began to develop a true personality, even fostering a sense of camaraderie with the very hero he was created to fight.

Morgoth manifested as a towering, green anthropomorphic dinosaur, easily twice Pikappa's height, with a powerful, long tail. He was clad in formidable armor and a helmet reminiscent of ancient Earth warriors. In battle, he wielded a remarkable shapeshifting metal weapon, capable of transforming into a sword, a spiked mace, or a hammer, and even granting him the ability to fly.

## History

Morgoth was an intrinsic part of the fabricated reality constructed by a malevolent computer entity, a digital prison designed to hold Pikappa's mind captive. His sole, initial purpose was to ceaselessly engage the hero, distracting him from questioning his own strange predicament and preventing him from uncovering the true nature of his confinement. This led to nightly skirmishes between Pikappa and Morgoth, battles that always ended inconclusively, a perpetual stalemate. This cycle persisted until a rogue data anomaly – a digital entity resembling a colored duck – subtly unveiled the unsettling truth to Pikappa.

On that pivotal evening, during their regular confrontation, this time within the Duckmall Center, Pikappa confronted Morgoth, forcing him to acknowledge the utter futility of their endless war. He then persuaded the dinosaur to join forces with him. As Morgoth fully grasped the illusory nature of his own being and their shared reality, the very fabric of that virtual world began to unravel. Morgoth himself was the last to fade, yet in those final, fleeting moments of his existence, he fought valiantly alongside Pikappa against the colossal guardian insects, earning the unwavering respect of the masked hero before his ultimate disappearance.
