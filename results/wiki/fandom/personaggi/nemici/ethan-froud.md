# Ethan Froud

Ethan Froud: A powerful telepath who stood against PK during the crisis at Crywater Dam. He relentlessly pursued Jana, a woman of equally formidable psychic abilities.

## Carattere e aspetto fisico

Ethan possessed powers mirroring Jana's—telekinesis and mind reading—yet his strength eclipsed hers, coupled with a distinct lack of self-control. His egomania was absolute; he held common humanity in contempt, even his own father, Director Max Froud, whose commands he barely tolerated before his ultimate defiance. His ambition was nothing less than a world where telepaths reigned supreme, akin to the gods of ancient myths. Paradoxically, his feelings for Jana, though intensely possessive, were undeniably genuine, and in his own twisted way, he sought to protect her from his father's influence.

Physically, Ethan was a tall and unsettling figure, yet powerfully built. His true, mutated nature was betrayed by subtle but striking alterations: long, prematurely graying hair, eyes that glowed yellow and lacked pupils, and lips stained green. He typically favored a dark, tailored suit, sometimes concealing his disturbing features behind a violet mask.

## Storia

Ethan is the son of Director Max Froud, the enigmatic head of The Factory, a highly classified governmental body dedicated to the study and exploitation of ESP abilities. Their familial bond, however, remained a closely guarded secret until the climax of their conflict. Under his father's tutelage, Ethan honed his nascent abilities at a clandestine base on a remote Greek island. It was there he encountered Jana and became utterly fixated on her. While Jana initially returned his affections, she soon recoiled upon witnessing his true nature—his insatiable ambition and dominating will—prompting her desperate escape from both him and The Factory.

Driven by his father's orders and leading a Factory team, Ethan relentlessly tracked Jana to Paperopoli. The desperate chase culminated at the Crywater Dam, where a cataclysmic telepathic duel unfolded. PK, empowered by Jana's transferred abilities, managed to repel Ethan's devastating assaults. Enraged by his defeat and consumed by a vicious jealousy towards Jana, Ethan finally defied Max. In a blinding fit of psionic fury, he unleashed his full power, causing the catastrophic destruction of the dam itself.

It was widely believed Ethan perished, swallowed by the raging river after he defiantly refused his father's outstretched hand. Yet, there remains a chilling uncertainty. Later, Jana herself confessed to sensing a distinct "Sì" reverberating within her mind—a psychic echo, leading many to believe it was Ethan's voice, hinting at his impossible survival.
