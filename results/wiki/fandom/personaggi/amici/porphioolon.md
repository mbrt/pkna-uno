Porphioolon, sometimes referred to as Porphyoolon, was an Evronian of singular importance, serving as the assistant to the nefarious Technician Manootensyon. He stands as the sole known example of an Evronian possessing integral goodness, a trait virtually unknown within their species.

## Nature and Physical Appearance

Beyond his exceptional scientific intellect, Porphioolon harbored qualities such as altruism and self-sacrifice, traits utterly alien to the Evronian psyche. He meticulously concealed these attributes by feigning incompetence and a perpetually mournful disposition. Even Manootensyon, his creator, remained oblivious to his true nature, utilizing him solely for menial tasks. Despite his radical deviation, Porphioolon maintained a paradoxical sense of solidarity with his own kind. His physical appearance was typical of a low-caste Evronian, distinguished by a particularly pronounced lower beak, lending him a deceptively dull countenance. He donned the standard blue robe of Evronian scientists.

## History

Porphioolon's existence was the accidental outcome of an experiment by Manootensyon, who sought to cultivate an emperor from a spore, capable of leading the surviving Evronian community. However, due to the technician's lack of foresight or understanding, Porphioolon developed profound goodness and morality instead of the desired leadership qualities. Recognizing that such traits would be anathema within Evronian society, Porphioolon adopted a guise of dim-wittedness, enduring Manootensyon's insults and abuse with remarkable patience.

When the Xerbian warrior Xadhoom fell comatose into Evronian hands, Porphioolon was tasked with examining her neural patterns. He quickly ascertained her identity as a formidable adversary of the Evronian Empire and, in an act of defiance, concealed this critical information from his peers, thus saving her life. Later, under the pretense of following Bonton and Manootensyon's orders, he successfully revived the prisoner.

Once his true nature was revealed to PK and Xadhoom, Porphioolon forged a desperate pact: he would aid the Xerbian in restoring her powers to defend Earth from the En'tomek, provided that his fellow Evronians were merely reduced to spores rather than annihilated. Even when Manootensyon uncovered the plot and Xadhoom faced execution, Porphioolon remained steadfast to his commitment. He activated the device that transformed all Evronians into spores, knowing this fate also awaited him. As the Evronian planetoide plunged towards a star, Xadhoom, in a final act of compassion, salvaged the spore of the unique Evronian. PK then transported this spore to Earth, entrusting it to Solomon Hicks in the hope of restoring Porphioolon to his original, sentient form.

The existence of Porphioolon's spore became known to the shapeshifting Evronian Grrodon, a self-proclaimed former emperor. He infiltrated the Ducklair Tower's lower levels, leveraging the inadequate security of Hicks's systems, which had been compromised while under Korinna Ducklair's influence. Grrodon's objective was to recover the spore, clone it, and thus rebuild his forces. It is believed that he was unaware of other surviving Evronian spores or prisoners held by Earth's Dept. 51, though some theorize these may have since been eradicated or repatriated into space. Regardless, Grrodon's plan was thwarted by the timely intervention of Pikappa, aided by the sentient AI UNO and the time-agent Lyla Lay.
