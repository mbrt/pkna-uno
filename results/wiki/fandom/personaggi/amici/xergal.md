# Commander Xergal

Commander Xergal is a pivotal figure in the history of our people. He led us through the long exile, establishing the haven of New Xerba, and stood firm against the Evronian threat even when we believed all hope was lost.

## Our Commander's Demeanor and Appearance

Commander Xergal embodies the very spirit of Xerba. He is revered by all, a leader both resolute and deeply contemplative, possessing an indomitable will. Though the years of exile have undeniably wearied him, he steadfastly conceals his own burdens, ensuring our community never falters in hope.

Despite his advanced years and smaller stature compared to many of our kin, his presence is immense. His head, though bare, is framed by a magnificent, full white beard and sideburns. He always wears the traditional long tunic in the proud colors of Xerba – white and violet – complemented by striking red gloves.

## His Journey

When our beloved Xerba fell to the Evronians, it was Commander Xergal who orchestrated the escape aboard the starship Xenia, saving countless lives. He navigated us to a desolate yet secure world, far from the reach of the invaders. Under his wise stewardship, our new home, New Xerba, flourished despite its harshness, as he guided us in maximizing every scarce resource.

Later, when the Evronian Emperor's forces discovered New Xerba, Commander Xergal, facing overwhelming odds, made the heartbreaking decision to surrender to prevent further bloodshed. Yet, even in captivity, his genius shone brightest. He devised the critical device that nullified the insidious effects of coolflamization, a breakthrough that proved instrumental in securing our ultimate victory.
