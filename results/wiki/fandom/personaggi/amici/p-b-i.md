# D.B.I.

The Duckburg Bureau of Investigation, known by its acronym D.B.I., is Calisota's foremost intelligence agency. Its primary responsibility is to acquire and analyze critical information pertaining to foreign governments, corporations, and individuals, subsequently reporting these vital findings to various branches of the Calisota government.

Our central command is located within Duckburg, at the Quacktico headquarters.

The D.B.I. has been critically involved in numerous key events, notably playing a significant role alongside Pikappa since the "Earthquake" incident. Our agents have also engaged in operations predating these events, such as those detailed in the "What Happened to Brigitta?" case in 1998.

Lieutenant Mary Ann Flagstarr is among our most recognized agents.

## Agents

### Active Duty

*   **Alma Leopoldine Courier:** The current Director of the D.B.I. She is known for her strict and unyielding approach with subordinates, yet possesses a keen ability to recognize the true worth of agents who dedicate themselves to national security.
*   **Mary Ann Flagstarr:** A brilliant agent, promoted to Lieutenant after the regrettable events during the protection mission of Professor Morgan Fairfax. She is the agent who collaborates most frequently with Pikappa.
*   **Malice Sturling:** An agent who assumed Mary Ann Flagstarr's duties when she was deployed overseas during the PK² operations.
*   **Merrywather:** A D.B.I. agent (security classification A2, lower than Flagstarr). Primarily handles office work... and coffee runs.
*   **Nimrod:** Mary Ann's superior.
*   **Paperelle:** An old acquaintance of Brigitta McBridge from their childhood. She first came into contact with our operations during the "What Happened to Brigitta?" case, later playing a more significant role in "Brigitta and the Mystery of Paperelle" in 2000.

### Former Members

*   **Brigitta McBridge:** Became a D.B.I. agent, influenced by her friend Paperelle, during the "What Happened to Brigitta?" case, where she was assigned the codename Agent 0010. By the conclusion of that operation, she chose to leave her career as a secret agent and returned to her previous life.

## Missions

### Concluded Successfully

*   **Operation Tyrannic:** Neutralizing the military threat orchestrated by the Republic of Belgravia (PKNA #21 Tyrannic).
*   **Salvage of Paperinik:** Successful retrieval of Paperinik (PKNA #29 Virus).
*   **Operation Hephaestus:** Thwarting a new military threat organized by the Republic of Belgravia (PKNA #45 Operation Hephaestus).

### Failed Operations

*   **Project Pangea:** Physical protection of Professor Morgan Fairfax and surveillance of his scientific work (PKNA #4 Terremoto).
*   **Missing:** Unsuccessful recovery operation of Paperinik (PKNA Speciale 97: Missing).
*   **Operation Falling Star:** Failure to identify and capture Xadhoom (PKNA #17 Stella Cadente).
*   **Capture of Tyrrel Duckard:** A dangerous criminal who escaped from Duckburg prison (PK² 5 La fine della storia).

## Headquarters

The Quacktico serves as the D.B.I.'s secure headquarters, first revealed during the "Missing" operation in '97. It is situated on the outskirts of Duckburg, nestled in a lush, green area. The facility is surrounded by extensive parkland and comprises three main light-brown buildings connected by smaller structures. Its entrance features a grand avenue leading to a reflective wall adorned with numerous national flags.
