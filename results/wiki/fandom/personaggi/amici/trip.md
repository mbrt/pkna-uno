# Trip

Trip, son of the infamous Razziatore, is a figure who has crossed paths with many. While initially involved in various minor temporal disruptions, he later became central to more significant events, notably those concerning Odin Eidolon, the Razziatore's unusual foray into conventional parenting, and the illusory manipulations of Korinna Ducklair.

## Appearance and Character

Trip is a young, anthropomorphic raptor, bearing a strong resemblance to the Razziatore, though he lacks his father's cybernetic enhancements – for now, that is, as whispers of his possible future self, the dreaded Grifone, suggest a darker, mechanized path. He's often seen in a vibrant green shirt and blue trousers, with unruly, fiery red hair. A small chronovela, usually strapped to his wrist, is a constant companion. His personality is as spirited as his hair: exuberant, mischievous, and often rebellious, yet beneath that, he's undeniably tender, lively, charming, generous, precocious, and remarkably intelligent. He harbors an intense passion for fictional heroes, often immersing himself in their adventures.

## Key Encounters

### Early Days

Trip first came to widespread attention during a period when the Razziatore, surprisingly, attempted to establish a more conventional relationship with his son. It was then that he gifted Trip his very first chronovela. While the Razziatore hoped it would be used responsibly, the young Trip, like many adolescents, quickly discovered its potential for personal convenience, leading to a series of chaotic, often humorous, temporal mishaps and frequent defiance of his father's wishes.

Later, during the "Nothing Personal" incident, Trip found himself rescued by Odin Eidolon, who sought to prevent him from becoming the dreaded chronopirate known as the Grifone – a dark future that Eidolon, aided by Pikappa, successfully averted. Following this pivotal event, during a period when Trip was staying at Paperinik's home, he channeled his boundless energy into creating his own comic. This personal project featured Timeboy, a young superhero who "steals time from the rich to give to the poor," a clear alter ego for Trip himself.

Trip reappeared during the "Time and Again" events, though in a less central role. It was during this time that the Razziatore made the unusual decision to temporarily abandon his criminal enterprises, choosing instead to focus on fatherhood.

### Recent Events

Most recently, during "The Horizon of Events," Trip made a fleeting appearance, albeit as a mere hallucination. Korinna Ducklair, Everett's eldest daughter, tormented the Razziatore by conjuring an image of Trip, making him believe Pikappa was stealing his son. This revealed a profound truth: the Razziatore's greatest fear, much like Pikappa's own, centered on the loss or manipulation of his offspring.
