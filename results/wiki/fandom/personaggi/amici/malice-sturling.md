# Malice Sturling

Malice Sturling is a highly effective PBI agent, who took over from Mary Ann Flagstarr when the latter departed for a lengthy overseas mission.

## Character and Physical Appearance

While she shares Mary Ann's tenacious spirit and professional competence, Malice Sturling is distinctly different from her predecessor due to her overtly hostile attitude towards PK. Not only does she go to great lengths to exclude the masked hero from her investigations, but she also constantly barrages him with sarcastic and scornful remarks.

Physically, Malice is a striking woman, despite her often abrasive personality. Thin and of average height, she has piercing green eyes and fiery red hair. She invariably wears the standard PBI uniform—a green jacket, black shirt and trousers, and a blue-visored cap—another point of contrast with Mary Ann, who typically operated undercover.

## History

Malice Sturling first made her mark during the events of "The End of History," where she led the unit tasked with apprehending Tyrrel Duckard. It was during this operation that she had her initial, confrontational encounter with PK. In "Blackout," Malice collaborated closely with Everett Ducklair to identify the culprit behind the mysterious sabotage of Ducklair Enterprises' computer network (later revealed to be Cormack Trentor). She strongly advocated for drastic measures, actions that nearly had severe consequences for PK. In "The Last Hunt," it was her intervention, prompted by an alert from Lyonard D'aq, that led to the arrest of the ruthless duo, Elias Fog and Ed Morando. Most recently, during "Family Matters," Malice took charge of the investigation into the supposed accident that seemingly claimed Everett's life.
