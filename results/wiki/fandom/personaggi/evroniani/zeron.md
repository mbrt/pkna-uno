# Zeron

Zeron, a pivotal figure in the ongoing conflict, first emerged during the critical period chronicled as "Sul lato oscuro". He holds the prestigious title of scientific branch leader and is a member of the Imperial Council, having assumed the regency of the Empire following the devastation of our homeworld, Evron.

## Aspetto fisico

Zeron displays the typical physical characteristics of an Evronian scientist. He wears a long, sea-green tunic, adorned with white lapels. Notably, his vampirized energy reservoirs are significantly larger than those seen on other technicians.

Zeron is among the few elder Evronians observed. Given our species' extended lifespan compared to Terrans, it is presumed the Counselor has exceeded 300 years of age. He possesses a long, unkempt beard, a face deeply etched with wrinkles, and his occiput is mottled with multiple dark purple patches. Embedded on his forehead is a trinket bearing the symbol of the Evronian army, while his beak displays several decorative stripes.

## Carattere

Zeron is recognized as a shrewd and cunning political operator, seemingly capable of suppressing his innate Evronian pride and belligerence to secure Terran assistance. He assumed leadership of our people during an exceptionally delicate historical juncture, with his primary concern being the survival of our species at any cost. This commitment led him to forge agreements even with his former adversaries, the Terrans, and to place unwavering trust in Pikappa's judgment.

These decisions rendered him unpopular among the more extremist and militaristic factions of the Evronian ruling class. General Zentium, in particular, accused him of capitulation and relentlessly sought to sabotage the peace accords with the Terrans.

Despite gaining the trust and respect of the Terran military representatives and even Paperinik himself, Zeron's purported pacifism proved to be a mere façade. In reality, the scientific counselor is a master of deception and manipulation, capable of dissimulating without arousing suspicion. His true objective was to infiltrate Earth with his agent, the shapeshifter Grrodon, in order to destabilize the planet's power structures and render it more susceptible to future conquest, once the Empire had recovered and reorganized. Therefore, he is almost certainly an individual of exceptional perfidy, disloyalty, and cunning, a true embodiment of our race's most strategic qualities.

## Storia

Zeron is a high-ranking member of the caste, a scientific branch leader, and a member of the Imperial Council, asserting himself as the paramount scientific authority of Evron. He assumed the regency of the Empire following the devastating defeat inflicted upon Evronian forces by Xadhoom and the Xerbians, which resulted in the destruction of our homeworld, Evron, and the rebellion of the Coolflames across all conquered planets.

After that calamitous battle, Zeron took command of the now decimated Evronian populace. His stated objective was to return to the Mira Ceti star system, the cradle of Evronian civilization, but during the journey, circumstances forced him to land on Earth's moon due to depleted fuel reserves.

He sought assistance from the Terrans, offering in exchange technological advancements and a solemn pledge never again to attempt the conquest of Earth. Following initial hesitations from both parties, the agreement was ratified, allowing the Evronians to return to their quadrant of origin. However, Zeron covertly instructed the shapeshifter Grrodon to remain on Earth as an infiltrator.

Grrodon indeed succeeded in destabilizing Terran power dynamics and, remarkably, in re-establishing the Evronian Empire, both by generating new Evronians from residual spores on Earth and by rallying former representatives of the old empire, ultimately proclaiming himself the new emperor. It remains uncertain whether this development was part of Zeron's grand design or if Grrodon chose to seize all power for himself rather than remain subservient to the elder Imperial counselor. It is also unknown if Zeron is aware of Grrodon's actions, or if the shapeshifter eliminated his former master to remove any rivals.

## Osservazioni

He stands out as one of the most diabolically astute Evronians encountered thus far. In his (to date) singular major interaction, the triple-agent Zeron managed to deceive not only General Westcock and the Terran delegation, but also Paperinik and even Uno, in addition to his own generals and branch leaders like the seemingly naive Zentium and Zeleron.

He personally ratified the peace treaty between Earth and Evron alongside Pikappa, whom he hypocritically referred to as "an additional friend in the universe." Following the attempted conquest of Earth by Grrodon's forces, both Westcock and Paperinik likely acknowledged the complete breach of any potential pact or agreement by the Evronians. It must be noted, however, that neither appeared particularly surprised at the prospect of confronting aggressive Evronians, far from peaceful, and eager to invade the planet.

In light of his ultimate betrayal, all of Zeron's statements are now subject to scrutiny. The Imperial counselor is an unreliable narrator and an untrustworthy source. It remains unclear how much of what he declared—particularly regarding the state of the Evronian Empire, his own role as regent, and the intentions of the survivors—was truthful and how much constituted part of his elaborate deception.
